APEX SYNCHRONISATION RULES (critical — do not violate):

- waitForLoadState('networkidle') calls in BasePage, page classes, and
  component classes are INTENTIONAL. APEX fires AJAX region refreshes and
  dynamic actions after every navigation and form submission. Do NOT remove
  these calls.
- waitForApexReady() in BasePage guards APEX JS initialisation — keep it.
- If a networkidle wait is the suspected cause of flakiness, replace it with
  a more targeted wait (waitForResponse, waitForSelector, locator.waitFor())
  rather than deleting it.
- Never add bare page.waitForTimeout() calls.
- Dynamic APEX element IDs (P1_ADD_PRODUCT, P42_PID_ORG, etc.) must not be
  used as locators — use ARIA role, label, or placeholder instead.
